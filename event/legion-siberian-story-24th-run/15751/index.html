Legion: Siberian story, 24th run

📅 13. 02. 2020 — 16. 02. 2020

📍 Liberecký, Železný Brod

👥 56 hráčů

Web: http://legion.rolling.cz/

Description Legion: Siberian Story tells stories of war, homeland, heroism, and people in a time of crisis. It is aimed not only at those interested in the time of the First World War, but also at everyone who wants to experience strong stories of everyday people. Legion is a high-production historical larp by the Czech association Rolling. The game's story is set in Siberia in the time of the Russian Civil War in a unit of volunteers, separated from their army and forced to march through a no man's land to get to their countrymen and maybe also finally home. The players will take roles as fictional Czechoslovak Legionnaires and the civilians who joined them.
